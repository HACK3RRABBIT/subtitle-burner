Before you continue:

- First launch needs an internet connection. It downloads the speech/translation
  engine (PyTorch, Whisper models, etc.) - this is a one-time download of several
  gigabytes and can take a while depending on your connection. Later launches
  start in seconds.
- Make sure you have a few GB of free disk space for those downloads.
- This app bundles FFmpeg (https://ffmpeg.org), licensed under the GPL. FFmpeg's
  own license and source are available from the FFmpeg project; it is included
  here as a separate, unmodified program invoked by this app, not linked into it.
